# Evaluation Rubrics

## MLC Construction (10 points)

| Criteria | Excellent (9–10) | Good (7–8) | Fair (5–6) | Poor (0–4) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Identification of Links** | All eight links correctly identified and documented. | One link missing or mislabelled. | Several links missing or misidentified. | Minimal or no understanding of the MLC. |
| **BFO Typing** | Correctly associates each link with the appropriate BFO category. | Minor errors in BFO mapping. | Frequent errors in mapping. | No or incorrect BFO mapping. |
| **Clarity** | Clear, well‑organised presentation. | Mostly clear; minor organisational issues. | Somewhat confusing or disorganised. | Lacks structure and clarity. |

## Contradiction Diagnosis (10 points)

| Criteria | Excellent (9–10) | Good (7–8) | Fair (5–6) | Poor (0–4) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Identification of Failures** | Correctly identifies all structural failures in the scenario. | Misses one failure or misclassifies one type. | Misses multiple failures; some misclassification. | Fails to identify or classify contradictions. |
| **Justification** | Provides clear justification linking failures to MLC links and BFO categories. | Provides justification with minor gaps. | Justification is weak or incomplete. | Provides little or no justification. |

## IRACplus Writing (10 points)

| Criteria | Excellent (9–10) | Good (7–8) | Fair (5–6) | Poor (0–4) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Issue Identification** | Issue is framed clearly in structural terms. | Issue is clear but lacks structural framing. | Issue is vague or partially inaccurate. | Issue is missing or misunderstood. |
| **Rule Statement** | Accurately states the applicable rule and authority. | Minor omissions or inaccuracies. | Rule is incomplete or incorrect. | Rule is missing or incorrect. |
| **Analysis** | Thoroughly applies the rule to the facts, referencing each MLC link and contradictions. | Analysis is generally strong with minor omissions. | Analysis lacks depth; several links or contradictions ignored. | Analysis is minimal or incorrect. |
| **Conclusion** | Conclusion follows logically from analysis, with structural diagnosis. | Conclusion generally follows, but structural diagnosis may be weak. | Conclusion is weak or unsupported. | Conclusion is missing or inconsistent. |
